1. Information We Collect

RoDev Academy is built to require as little personal information as possible — ideally none.

  • No account required. All lessons, projects, and interactive tools are accessible without an account or personal information.
  • Lesson progress. Completed lessons are saved in your browser's localStorage only. Never transmitted to any server.
  • Voluntary contact. If you reach out via YouTube or GitHub, we receive only your public username and message.
  • No analytics. We run no tracking or analytics scripts whatsoever.

2. How We Use It

Lesson progress in localStorage is used only to display which lessons you have completed. It never leaves your device. Messages sent to us voluntarily are used only to respond to you — never shared or sold.

3. Cookies & Browser Storage

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save lesson progress. localStorage cannot be read by other websites and does not expire automatically. We set no first-party cookies. See our Cookie Policy for the full breakdown including third-party services.

5. Data Storage & Security

RoDev Academy runs no servers that store personal data. The platform is a static HTML/CSS/JS application served over HTTPS. All persistence lives exclusively in your browser's localStorage. If you discover a security vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly via our GitHub repository.

6. Children's Privacy

RoDev Academy is aimed at learners aged 10 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because no account is required, learners of any age can use the site without providing personal information. There is no user-generated content, comment system, or social features on the platform.

7. Your Rights

  • Right to deletion: Clear your browser's localStorage to permanently remove all lesson progress. No server request needed.
  • GDPR (EU/EEA): You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability. Contact us to exercise these.
  • CCPA (California): We do not sell personal data. Contact us for any CCPA request.

8. Changes to This Policy

Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
