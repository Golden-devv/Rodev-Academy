Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

RoDev Academy takes a minimal approach to cookies and browser storage. This page explains exactly what is stored in your browser, why, and how to remove it.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and collect analytics data.

There are different types of cookies:

  • Session cookies — deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies — remain until they expire or you delete them
  • First-party cookies — set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-party cookies — set by external services embedded on the page

2. Do We Use Cookies?

Short answer: No. RoDev Academy does not set any first-party cookies. We have no analytics, no advertising network, no login system, and no tracking of any kind.

We use localStorage instead of cookies to save your lesson progress. localStorage is a browser feature that works differently from cookies — see Section 3 for details.

3. localStorage (Not Cookies)

RoDev Academy uses your browser's localStorage API to save:

  • Which lessons you have marked as complete
  • Your current lesson position

Key differences between localStorage and cookies:

PropertylocalStorageCookies
Sent to server? No — stays on your device Yes — sent with every request
Expiry Until you clear browser data Set by the website (days/years)
Accessible by other sites? No Depends on settings
Used for tracking? Not in our case Commonly yes

The localStorage data we store contains only lesson IDs and completion states. No personal information is stored.

5. Full Storage Inventory

Here is a complete list of everything stored in your browser by RoDev Academy:

KeyTypePurposeExpires
rodev_progress localStorage Tracks which lessons are marked complete Until you clear browser data

That's it. One key. No tracking pixels, no session IDs, no fingerprinting.

6. How to Clear Your Data

To remove all RoDev Academy data from your browser:

Option A — Clear just RoDev data

Open your browser's developer tools (F12), go to Application → Local Storage → your site URL, and delete the rodev_progress key.

Option B — Clear all browser data

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files + Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data
Note: Clearing your browser data will reset your lesson progress. You'll need to re-mark completed lessons.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Cookie Policy if we add new features that involve browser storage or third-party services. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
